I burnt my croissant. What should I do?
Don't worry! You can scrape off the burnt parts and enjoy the rest. Next time, keep an eye on the oven temperature and cooking time.
Thank you! Do you have any tips for making croissants flakier?
To make your croissants flakier, make sure to fold the dough properly to create those beautiful layers. Also, use cold butter and let the dough rest in the refrigerator between folds to ensure it stays chilled.
How many times should I fold the croissant dough?
It depends on the recipe, but typically you fold the dough three times, giving it a total of four folds. This process helps create those flaky layers that croissants are known for.

Bavarian Donut Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of donut dough (see recipe)
  • 1 cup Bavarian cream filling
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the donut dough according to the recipe provided.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut it into donut shapes using a donut cutter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Fry the donuts until they are golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  5. Remove the donuts from the oil and let them drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  6. Prepare the Bavarian cream filling according to the recipe provided.
  7. Fill a piping bag fitted with a small round tip with the Bavarian cream.
  8. Insert the tip into the side of each donut and pipe the cream into the center.
  9. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make the glaze.
  10. Dip the tops of the filled donuts into the glaze and return them to the wire rack to set.
  11. If desired, decorate the glazed donuts with sprinkles while the glaze is still wet.
  12. Allow the glaze to set before serving the Bavarian donuts.

Sprinkled Donut Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of donut dough (see recipe)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Assorted sprinkles

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the donut dough according to the recipe provided.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut it into donut shapes using a donut cutter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy-bottomed pot to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Fry the donuts until they are golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  5. Remove the donuts from the oil and let them drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make the glaze.
  7. Dip the tops of the cooled donuts into the glaze, allowing any excess glaze to drip off.
  8. Immediately sprinkle the glazed donuts with assorted sprinkles before the glaze sets.
  9. Place the glazed and sprinkled donuts on a wire rack to set completely.
  10. Enjoy the delicious homemade sprinkled donuts!
